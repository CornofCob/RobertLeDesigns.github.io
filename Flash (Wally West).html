Flash

Wally West

Wally West Flash, Barry Allen’s successor, when first became the Flash pretty much just used his uncle’s costume. There are only a few minor differences, like the more metallic sheen, the slightly different lightning belt and the white lenses on his eyes.

I tried to adapt his Flash Rebirth suit in 2009 to differentiate the two Flashes (Flashs? Flush?) more without changing him too much. Which is why this Flash design has a cowl that covers his nose. Because of the metallic distinction in the comics, Wally’s suit here is made of thin sheets of metal, while Barry’s will be mostly cloth. The symbol and lightning patterns are spray-painted on the red suit to further accentuate the sleek and high tech aesthetic.

His ear lightning is also altered slightly, and the lenses are more reflective to add more personality, much like Alex Ross’ take on Spider-man.
