Green Lantern

John Stewart

There are many Green Lanterns from Earth in the source material, but only two have had the honor of being a core member of the Justice League, Hal Jordan and John Stewart. I choose John because he has been a recurring main member ever since the Justice League animated series, where I was introduced to the character. John’s comic book costume is also perfect for figuring out the general aesthetic for other Lanterns that I’m going to design down the line.

The character is often referred to as the Emerald Knight, so in that spirit the green parts of the suit are designed to look like the texture and sheen of emeralds. The patterns throughout the suit are regal to further accentuate the knight and light-based power elements. Because I want the Lanterns of Earth to have a uniform look, there are elements of other Green Lanterns on John’s costume, and elements of this suit will appear in other GL designs of mine.

Overall, it's pretty straightforward. The main idea was to not make it resemble any fabric, because the suit is a light-based construct, whatever that means.
