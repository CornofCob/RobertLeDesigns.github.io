Wasp

Janet Van Dyne

The design for the Wasp here is very classic, with only a bit more details. Though Wasp has gone through many costumes over the years, with red or yellow as her main color scheme, I picked the black and red original for Janet Van Dyne, and saved yellow for her daughter.

A red visor is added to her helmet to provide protection and serve as a slight reference to her movie appearance. Because the Wasp in the comics has an innate shrinking ability, she doesn’t need to use Pym particles, which means her belt is empty compared to Ant-man’s belt.

In the movies, Wasp’s signature wings are part of the suit, but in the source material they are synthetic wings planted on her body. Because of this I went with anatomically accurate wasp wings, something that I just realized might look quite gross in live action.
