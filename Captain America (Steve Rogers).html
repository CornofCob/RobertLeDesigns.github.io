Captain America

Steve Rogers

“I’ve been doing this all day.”

I think that’s how the quote goes. Anyway, it did take me a day to figure out what works best for the star-spangled man with a plan. The idea is to give him not just a militaristic look like in the movies, but actual military gear and clothing, because he is a soldier after all. Some movie fans may not know that before the Chris Evans version, Cap often worked quite close with the government and sometimes that still happens in modern iterations of the character. So this idea really works. In fact, they tried this for the somewhat disappointing Avengers PS4 game. But the execution I (and many) think was a bit lacking and they made the mistake of making him too bulky.

So here we have a red, white, and blue bulletproof vest, but made to be as slimming as possible while still remaining realistic. Red tactical gloves and boots are the way to go, while the shield is as classic as possible.

The mask resembles the one used in films and modern comics, but I made the wings on his temples pop a little bit more. There is a ballistic neck piece that is tucked under the helmet as well.

Underneath the vest is a white army shirt along with blue tactical pants. The type of tactical pants I chose here has lines around his crotch area as a nod to the fact that classic Cap looks like he wears a blue trunk outside of his blue pants (comics are weird, I know).

And there's always more to come!
