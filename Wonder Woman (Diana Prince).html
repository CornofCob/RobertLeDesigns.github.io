Wonder Woman

Diana Prince

The ancient Greek warrior herself! I try to amp up the Greek influences in this design while still staying true to the character. The body armor is very much modeled after a typical Greek hopliteâ€™s cuirass, complete with sculpted musculature and swirly patterns.

The tiara and symbol are kept fairly similar to the classic, while the metal wrist guards and boots are typical combat gear that an Amazon would wear to battle. Though the all - American part of the character has almost disappeared entirely in many different depictions of the character, the star - spangled hip armor and skirt actually works in this context.

Her body straps and weapons, aside from the famous Lasso of Truth, are all based on the actual Greek designs. The shield especially has some rather flimsy ropes that help wielder hold it in battle or simply wear it on their back.

And to be clear, my design for the Invisible Jet behind her is incredible, too bad no one can see it.
